👥 About Us

ABOUT
US

Sparkle Heaven was born from passion, friendship, and creativity. We are a team of energetic youngsters who turned our love for celebrations into a full-service event management company.

Based in Othakkadai, Madurai, our mission is simple — every occasion deserves to feel magical, memorable, and stress-free for you.

✨ Our Story

We Don't Plan Events…
We Craft Happiness

From intimate family functions to grand celebrations, we carefully plan, design, and execute every detail with perfection. We don't just arrange events… we create experiences that your guests will talk about long after the day ends.

Whether it's the emotional beauty of a wedding, the joy of a baby shower, the excitement of a surprise party, or the professionalism of a corporate event — we bring creativity, elegance, and smooth management to every moment.
